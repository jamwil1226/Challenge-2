About Me

Combining my love for design and technology with my organizational skills and attention to detail, web development is a perfect fit. In 2014, I started a non-profit organization to donate teddy bears to children in the hospital and needed a website. I decided to learn how to build a website on my own and discovered then my passion for web development. Several years later, I built a website to house a travel blog for my cross-country travels. The following year, I created a website to turn my then physical therapy career into a way of helping the elderly stay safe at home and reduce their fall risk.


I am hard-working, reliable, and pride myself on my organizational and communicational skills. I am very goal-oriented and excel in working with a team to achieve a successful outcome while meeting deadlines.


I would love an opportunity to tell you more about myself and answer any questions that you may have. I am actively looking for a web development position and would appreciate the opportunity to connect with you!
